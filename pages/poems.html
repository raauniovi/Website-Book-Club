Selected Poems

Beneath the quiet moonlight,
the river hums its ancient song,
carrying whispers of forgotten days.

The sun is warm, the sky is clear,
the waves are dancing free;
the birds are singing everywhere,
how happy we can be!

Love is the wine of the soul,
and the beloved is the cup.
Drink deeply, for this is life.

Morning Poem

The sun is warm, the sky is clear,
the waves are dancing free.

The birds are singing everywhere,
soft verses in the tree.

The breeze moves gently through the air,
how peaceful life can be.
